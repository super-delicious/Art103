Phase 2

I made two stamp models and created CAM toolpaths for each of the two stamp models. The first one is the letter Y which is my initial of first name. The font I choose to design in Illustrator is Minion Variable Concept. I like this elegant pen edge. I also designed a symbol that was my animated avatar. In the beginning, I made a mistake, since I added the detail of eyelashes and highlight, then all the model bodies did not combine together, although the CAM Toolpaths could be work in stimulation. As I subtracted the details of the eyes and merged all the body models, the CAM can Produce Toolpath better on my stamp model.
