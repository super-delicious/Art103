Part 2 -- Student Art Presentations


Social Distance in a Shelter


Maya 3D Modeling & Rendering, 1920x1080, 2020

My inspiration originates from the fact that the epidemic is a contagious disease that requires people to keep a social distance. This motivates me to create a virtual space as the scenery of spiritual healing for security purposes. It is also the revelation of the reality of environmental degradation. I proposed and completed a prosthetic architecture to stay inside a comfortable and safe environment, hoping to reduce the chances of people being exposed to the outside, which is harmful. My lighting tends to create sensory of blazing hot environment and a purplish-grey sky, aiming for constructing an atmosphere of recent California's fire.
